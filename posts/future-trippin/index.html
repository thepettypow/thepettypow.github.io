Future Trippin’

2024-12-05Petty Pow

Thinking About the Future is a Fucking Trip#

You ever just sit there, minding your own business, and BAM—your brain decides it’s time to think about the future? Yeah, same. It’s like getting sucker-punched by your own thoughts. One minute you’re chilling, the next you’re spiraling into this endless void of what ifs and holy shits.

Why It’s So Damn Exhausting#

Here’s the thing about thinking too much about the future: it’s a fucking rabbit hole. And not the cute Alice in Wonderland kind. It’s the oh shit, I’m free-falling with no end in sight kind.

  • It’s overwhelming AF. There’s just too much to consider. jobs, relationships, money, health. It’s like your brain decided to binge-watch every possible scenario of your life, but only the bad episodes.
  • You can’t turn it off. It’s 3 a.m., and instead of sleeping, you’re lying there thinking, “What if I fail? What if I’m wasting time? What if everything goes to shit?” It’s like your brain’s a broken record, and the song sucks.
  • Paralysis by overanalysis. You think you’re planning, but really, you’re just stressing yourself out to the point where you can’t even take the first step.

Why Do We Even Do This?#

Honestly? No clue. Maybe it’s some survival instinct gone rogue. Maybe it’s because we want control in a world that’s constantly flipping us the bird. Or maybe we’re just wired to torture ourselves. Whatever it is, it’s fucked up.

But here’s the kicker: thinking about the future isn’t always bad. It’s what pushes us to dream bigger, to work harder, to be better. It’s just that sometimes, it takes over, and instead of motivating us, it starts feeling like a fucking boulder crushing our chest.

How I’m Trying Not to Lose My shit#

Alright, so I’m not gonna pretend I’ve got this figured out. Spoiler: I don’t. But here’s what I’m trying to do to make it suck a little less:

  1. Focus on the now. Easier said than done, but sometimes you gotta remind yourself that the future starts with what you’re doing right now.
  2. Control what you can. Not everything’s in your hands, but the stuff that is? Own that shit.
  3. Let the rest go. Sounds cheesy as hell, but honestly, there’s freedom in just saying, “Fuck it, I’ll deal with that when it comes.”

So yeah, thinking about the future? Total mindfuck. But it’s also kinda what makes us human. It’s messy, it’s stressful, and it’s a pain in the ass, but maybe—just maybe—it’s worth it.

For now, though? I’m just trying to survive the ride without completely losing my mind.
